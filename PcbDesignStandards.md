## 1.1. General

- Snap Grid:        0.5000mm (19.69mils)
- Round Corners:    1mm Radius
- PCB Size:         1mm increments

## 1.2. Mounting Holes

- #10 Bolt
  - Non-Grounded & Non-Thermal: 5mm Mounting Hole, No Annular

## 1.3. Part Selection

- Resistors:  Surface mount 1206
